Can't Sleep? Try:

  1. Getting up and leaving the room for a bit
  2. Disconnecting from all gadgets before sleeping
  3. Distracting yourself from your thoughts by imagining scenarios in your head
  4. Meditating

Not being able to sleep may be caused by outside issues, such as your comfortability. A cool bedroom, comfortable mattress and sheets may improve your sleep.

If insomnia persists, you may need to speak with a doctor or a specialist.
