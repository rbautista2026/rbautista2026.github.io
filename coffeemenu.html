THE ALL-NIGHTER'S CAFE

Coffee

Plain Black Coffee

May be served hot or cold.

Espresso

A shot of coffee that is stronger and thicker due to its brewing method. Served hot.

Cappuccino

A drink with espresso and hot milk, topped with foamed milk. Served hot.

Macchiato

An espresso shot with a small amount of foamed milk on top. Served hot.

Mocha

Espresso and hot milk flavored with chocolate. May be served either hot or cold.

.

Tea

Black Tea

Black tea served hot. Choose between English Breakfast, a blend of different black teas, or Earl Grey, a black tea infused with bergamot to give it a citrus flavor.

Green Tea

Green tea served either hot. Choose between sencha tea, which is made by infusing the leaves into water, or matcha, which is made by grinding the leaves into a powder and then mixing it into the water.

Milk Tea

Milk tea with added chewy boba pearls. Available in Wintermelon, Brown Sugar, and Taro.
