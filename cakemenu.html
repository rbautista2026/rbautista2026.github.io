THE ALL-NIGHTER'S CAFE

Cake

Coffee Cake

Not a cake made with coffee, but rather a cake made for eating with coffee. Vanilla sponge cake with a cinnamon sugar streusel topping.

Tiramisu

Ladyfingers dipped in coffee, layered with mascarpone cheese and dusted with cocoa powder on top.

Cheesecake

A cake (pie?) with a graham cracker crust and cream cheese base. Toppings may include fruit or nuts depending on order.

Pastries

Donuts

Donuts, available in plain glazed, chocolate, and jelly-filled.

Cinnamon Rolls

Buns filled with cinnamon sugar and topped with cream cheese frosting.

Croissants and Danishes

Croissants are famed for their crescent-like shape. A similar pastry, the danish, is flaky and buttery, which are available with fruit or cream cheese on top.
