Before going to bed...

Prepare for the next day.

Check if you have anything to prepare for the next day. If you have to, set an alarm if you are to wake early the next day. Practice a consistent sleep schedule to have better rest.

Clean Yourself.

Change into some comfortable clothes, wash your face, and brush your teeth.

Some people also have an extensive skincare routine, that thoroughly cleans and nourishes their skin.

Make Your Surroundings Comfortable.

Dim the lights, open a window to cool down your room. Make sure the room is free of noise and distractions. Some people prefer to listen to music as they sleep, which is fine.

Other activities people do before sleeping

  1. Read a book
  2. Meditate
  3. Have a snack, or a drink
  4. Stretch
