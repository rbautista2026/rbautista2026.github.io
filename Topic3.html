The Importance Of Sleep

During sleep, your body:

  1. Replenishes energy
  2. Repairs cells
  3. Produces hormones, like those needed for growth

Without adequate sleep, you:

  1. May feel tired
  2. May not be able to concentrate or focus on your activities
  3. Might struggle to do certain physical activities
  4. Weaken your immune system

Sleep is essential for a functioning body, and a typical person requires about 7-9 hours for a full night's rest.
